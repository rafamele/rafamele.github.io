Rafael Ramirez-Melendez

Hi! I'm an Associate Professor and Leader of the Music and Machine Learning Lab at the Department of Information and Communication Technologies, Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona. I obtained my BSc in Mathematics from the National Autonomous University of Mexico, and my MSc and PhD in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Bristol, UK. I studied Classical Violin and Guitar and have played with different music groups in Europe, America and Asia. My research interests include music technology, artificial intelligence, and their application to health and well-being.
